# Project Scope

## Overview

StayMatch is a web-based student housing and roommate compatibility platform designed to assist students in finding verified accommodations and compatible roommates when moving to a new city for higher education.

The system aims to simplify the accommodation search process by providing personalized recommendations, compatibility-based roommate matching, and decision-support features such as reviews, ratings, and comparisons.

---

# In Scope

The following functionalities are included in the proposed system:

## 1. User Management

- Student registration and login.
- Property owner registration and login.
- Profile management for students and owners.
- Secure authentication and authorization.

## 2. Accommodation Management

- Create, update, and delete accommodation listings.
- Upload property images and details.
- Browse and view accommodation information.
- Search and filter accommodations.

## 3. Roommate Compatibility System

- Lifestyle preference questionnaire.
- Living Compatibility Index (LCI) calculation.
- Compatibility-based roommate matching.

## 4. Recommendation System

- Personalized accommodation recommendations.
- Suggested roommate recommendations.

## 5. Reviews and Ratings

- Submit reviews and ratings for accommodations.
- View reviews and ratings from other students.

## 6. Favorites and Comparison

- Save favorite listings.
- Compare multiple accommodations.

## 7. Communication System

- Real-time chat between students and property owners.
- Chat between potential roommates.

## 8. Administration

- User management.
- Listing verification.
- Review moderation.
- Dashboard and analytics.

---

# Out of Scope

The following functionalities are not included in the current version of the project:

- Online rent payment and transactions.
- Booking and reservation management.
- Legal agreement generation.
- Integration with third-party real estate platforms.
- Native Android or iOS applications.
- Machine Learning-based recommendation engine.
- Video calling and voice communication.
- GPS tracking and navigation services.

---

# Project Boundaries

- The system is intended primarily for students seeking accommodation.
- Compatibility scores are based on user-provided information and may not perfectly predict real-life roommate relationships.
- Property verification depends on information provided by owners and administrative review.
- Recommendations are generated using predefined rules and preferences rather than advanced machine learning techniques.

---

# Future Scope

The platform can be extended in the future by incorporating:

- AI and Machine Learning recommendations.
- Mobile applications.
- Online booking and payment integration.
- Email and push notifications.
- Advanced analytics and reporting.
- Group roommate matching.
- Integration with mapping and nearby services.
- Fraud detection and verification mechanisms.

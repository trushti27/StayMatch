# Problem Statement

## Background

Every year, thousands of students relocate to different cities for higher education and face significant challenges in finding suitable accommodation. The process of searching for a place to live is often time-consuming, stressful, and lacks reliable information. Students not only need affordable and safe housing but also require compatible roommates to ensure a comfortable living environment.

## Existing Problems

Current housing platforms primarily focus on property listings and provide limited support for student-specific requirements. The major challenges faced by students include:

- Difficulty in finding verified and trustworthy accommodation.
- Lack of information regarding property quality and living conditions.
- Difficulty in finding compatible roommates with similar lifestyles and preferences.
- Time-consuming comparison of multiple accommodation options.
- Limited decision support and personalized recommendations.
- Increased chances of roommate conflicts due to incompatible habits and expectations.

## Problem Statement

Students moving to a new city for higher education often struggle to find safe, affordable, and verified accommodation along with compatible roommates. Existing housing platforms mainly provide property listings and fail to address student-centric needs such as roommate compatibility analysis, personalized recommendations, and informed decision-making support. As a result, students frequently experience unsuitable living arrangements, roommate conflicts, misinformation about properties, and an inefficient housing search process.

## Proposed Solution

StayMatch aims to address these challenges by developing a student-centric housing and roommate compatibility platform that:

- Provides verified PG and rental accommodation listings.
- Helps students discover compatible roommates using a Living Compatibility Index (LCI).
- Offers personalized recommendations based on preferences and requirements.
- Enables informed decision-making through reviews, ratings, and comparison features.
- Facilitates communication between students and property owners through an integrated chat system.

## Expected Impact

The proposed system is expected to:

- Reduce the time and effort required to find suitable accommodation.
- Improve the overall housing experience for students.
- Minimize roommate conflicts by promoting compatibility-based matching.
- Increase trust through verified listings and user reviews.
- Provide a centralized platform tailored specifically to student housing needs.
